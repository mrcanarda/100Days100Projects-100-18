All new features and updates in Live 12

To see highlights of the release in more detail, visit the Live 12 page

Play freely with ideas

MIDI Transformations

Create a variety of simple or complex variations to your MIDI clips_ add ornaments and articulations, draw acceleration and decelerations curvers, connect successive note and chords, or simulate the strum oa a guitar

MIDI Generators

Conjure up melodies, chords and rhytms with new MIDI Generators. Give your chosen Generator custon constrains, watch it create playful and original patterns, and then turn the ideas it produces into your own

Max for Live MIDI Tools

Max now faciliates even greater expreimentation, letting you create new MIDI Transformations and Generators - either based on exisiting ones, or your own creations.

MIDI Editing Improvements

Arrange the order of a selections of notes by pitch, velocity or duration. Stretch, split, chop or join notes in new ways; shape the velocity of notes; transpose them into a scale; or add intervals chromatically or in key.

Keys and Scales

Set the selected clip's scale in Live's Control Bar to see its notes in any clip you create. Then edit the clip scale highlithg as a guide, use the clip scale to transfor and generace musical ideas, or sync the scale of MIDI devices and intruments to the clip being played. Any changes in Live will be reflected in Push, and vice versa.

Expanded probabilty

Assing a single probabilty rule to a grpoup of notes so that the entire group of notes is played whenever the trigger is fired. Or select a chord and tell Live randomly play one note from it whenever the probabilty trigger is fired.
